TextBoard
1452044868 Anonymous
A textboard is a special kind of Japanese forum that is more like a guestbook than traditional forum software.

Examples of TextBoard's:

- KareHa (most popular textboard software in the west)


- TwoChannel (largest forum in the world)
- WorldTwoCh (alive, vintage)
- WorldFourCh (dead)
- SaOvq (resurrected)
- SageruOrg by MeltingWax
- TableCat
- IyagiBbs by FourByThirteen
