It is common for people on the InterNet to have mental illness. Perhaps there is something about it which can exacerbate conditions. Most serious mental illness relates to a person's biology, while some are learned behaviors. A primary symptom of most mental illness is stress.

- Anxiety
- Depression
- ADHD
- Bisexuality

- Alcoholism
- Paranoia
- Agoraphobia
- MagicalThinking
- Narcissm

- Depersonalization disorder
- PTSD
